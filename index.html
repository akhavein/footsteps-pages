Local-first by design

Privacy without caveats.

Footsteps is a privacy-first iOS app that automatically tracks where you go and helps you journal your journeys. Your data stays on your device. There are no third-party analytics, no ad SDKs, and no cloud backend operated by Footsteps.

Read the privacy policy Contact support

What the app does

Footsteps helps you keep a personal location journal with on-device visit detection, route history, saved places, and activity insights. Its privacy model is simple: the product works without collecting or storing your personal data on remote servers.

Privacy highlights

  • No analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs.
  • Location and motion data stay on your device.
  • Reverse geocoding relies only on Apple's built-in services.
