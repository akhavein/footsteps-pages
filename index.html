A privacy-first site that looks like the app.

Footsteps is a local-first location journal built around the same visual language as the app itself: crisp system typography, strong launch-screen blue, and the route shield icon that anchors the brand. Your data stays on your device.

Footsteps Your private location journal
  • All visit history and routes stay on-device.
  • No analytics, ad SDKs, or Footsteps cloud backend.
  • Apple-only reverse geocoding for readable places.

What the app does

Footsteps helps you keep a personal location journal with on-device visit detection, route history, saved places, and activity insights. The app uses Apple system UI and a simple color story: blue for navigation and action, white for readable content, and the green route icon as the signature brand mark.
