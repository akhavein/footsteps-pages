Private location journal for iPhone.

Footsteps helps you keep a personal record of where you go without handing that data to anyone else. Visits, routes, saved places, and activity history stay on your device.

What the app does

Footsteps automatically detects visits, records travel routes, and organizes your places and activity history in one timeline. It is designed to work without analytics, advertising, or a Footsteps-operated cloud service.

Automatic visit detection and daily timeline
Route history and activity insights
Saved places with names, categories, and tags
Export and delete controls inside the app

Privacy-first by design

  • All location and activity history stays on your device.
  • No analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs.
  • No Footsteps cloud backend storing your trips.
  • Apple-only reverse geocoding for readable place names.
